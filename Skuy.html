What is Skuy?

Skuy is an iOS and Android app to help college students connect with their campus. With a team of students, we have built and offer diverse public communities, campus dining hall and news trackers, and an event registry for all campus events. Learn more down below!

How are you involved?

Role: Director of Product Management
Time: February 2021 - Present
Tools: Jira, Excel, Figma, Firebase, Slack

The Problem

Skuy began in Northwestern’s start-up incubator “The Garage.” Myself and a couple other interested students set out to solve a number of problems campus faced such as:

“Campus feels so disconnected”

“How do I find people interested in the same topics as me?”

“What do I use to find out what’s happening on campus?”

Skuy became our solution

My Role

I serve as one of two Product Managers for Skuy, leading everything platform related. By platform, I mean everything related to user research, feature ideation and decisions, design directions, and development.

My philosophy when working on Skuy is:

- Always do discovery on what features our users WANT and NEED

- Iterate, iterate, and iterate more on our designs

- Build our features in sustainable ways that allow us to leverage that work for later development
