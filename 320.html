Industrial Design Methods

Object Design + Sketching

Much of my early work revolved around taking deep dives into various objects to better understand shape and form, first starting off with quick, in-the-moment thumbnails. These thumbnails eventually involved into larger sketches that utilized precise line-work and shading.

Perspective Design + Sketching

The second half of my industrial design endeavor focused on developing perspective sketching. I worked on perspectives such as bird’s eye, eye level, and worm’s eye with additional factors such as lighting and shadows, with projects being bus stops and perspective objects.
